I keep getting this error, but have orders to submit!

webDiplomacy runs on shared hosting as a hobbyist project, and unfortunately occasional problems like this are an inevitable consequence, but systems are in place to reduce the impact:

The GameMaster keeps track of when it was last active (usually less than 5 minutes), and will suspend all processing when it detects that it has been offline for more than 30 minutes.
This means that if the system has been down for more than 30 minutes games will not move onto the next turn until time has been added to compensate for the downtime, or the game has been paused for live games etc.

So if you have been seeing only this page despite checking every few minutes for half an hour you don't need to worry about your games continuing without your orders when webDiplomacy comes back online.

However accounting for unexpected problems is always an imperfect process, and unaffected players/games do not want unnecessarily long time extensions; if you have orders to submit try frequently to submit them, don't rely on the safety net if you don't need to. Due to the nature of the game turns cannot be rewound for players which missed them no matter what the circumstances!

Sorry for the inconvenience, we hope to be back online as soon as possible.
